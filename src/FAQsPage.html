About the System

You can manage all your computer laboratory reservations through a single dashboard. The system allows you to view available labs, select time slots, track your current and past reservations, and receive confirmation notifications instantly.

Yes. The system is designed for authorized students, faculty members, and staff. Users must log in using their institutional credentials to ensure secure and proper use of laboratory resources.

Reserving a laboratory means you are securing access to a specific computer lab at a chosen date and time. This helps prevent scheduling conflicts, ensures fair access, and allows the university to manage lab usage efficiently.

If your selected laboratory or time slot is unavailable, you can choose from other available labs or time periods. You may also check back later, as availability updates in real time when reservations are modified or canceled.
